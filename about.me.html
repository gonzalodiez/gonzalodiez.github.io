Passionate about people and technology

Hello world, my name is Gonzalo and I am 29 years old. I grew up in a small town called Tandil in Argentina, and studied Chemical Engineering at the National University of La Plata.

I am a sociable and charismatic person, and I value being a good teammate. I come from a small family and have always enjoyed puzzles and riddles as a way to keep my mind sharp. I am always eager to learn new things and take on new challenges, which is why I recently decided to study data analytics and artificial intelligence. I am particularly interested in how these technologies will shape the future, and I want to be at the forefront of their development.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I am also passionate about management and self-improvement. I actively seek out ways to improve my communication and leadership skills, and I enjoy reading about how to positively influence others. I value my relationships and make an effort to spend quality time with the people who are important to me. My travels have also given me a greater appreciation for different cultures and a broader perspective on life. Overall, I am a well-rounded and driven individual with a passion for learning and personal growth.
